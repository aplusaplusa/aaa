Design
Clinic

Design Clinic offers individual consultations for design renovation services. We help demystify the design process, develop a clear roadmap, and gather the information you need to prepare for your next home, office, retail, or other space—whether it’s an alteration, renovation, or addition.

The Design Clinic package includes:
  • A 60-minute session with an A+A+A design expert to ask questions, discuss your project vision and goals, and break down the design and construction process.
Project-guiding information, including:
  • • Project feasibility
  • • Budgeting and minimum cost investments
  • • Timeline
  • • Architect and project team needs
  • • Zoning and building permit requirements

A custom, tailor-made worksheet summarizing your session, with clear, actionable next steps.
